Our Story

Saaram is an artisanal coffee experience crafted with love and patience. Each batch is roasted in small drums, guided by instinct and years of soulful brewing. From carefully sourced beans to mindful packaging, every detail celebrates warmth, hospitality, and the comfort of a perfect cup.

We are soulfully owned and nurtured by Krishna Kaapi Works, nestled in the heart of Nallagandla. Saaram is our ode to the community that inspired us to bottle the aroma of home.
